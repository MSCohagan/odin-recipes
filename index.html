Cohagan Cooking Challenge
Rules

Ladies and gentlemen! Welcome to the official website of the annual Cohagan Cooking Challenge! This challenge will be taking place over the course of October 2021 (8/1/21-8/31/21). Participants are welcome to come from anywhere, however the challenge will be held in Ajkl, Cerra. We have rented out a space in the Ajkl Performing Arts center for daily taste testing. Your dish will be ranked on a scale of 1-10. This score will be secret until the end of the month when all scores will be revealed to the general public. The best 3 dishes will be awarded with cash prizes amounting to 5,000 gobbledygooks, 1,000 gobbledygooks, and 500 gobbledygooks.

Cash prizes will be presented to winners in person. If you are not present at the award ceremony on November 5th, 2021 your prize will be given to the next highest scoring contestant. If extenuating circumstances barre you from attending (sickness, plague, the end of the world, etc.) please submit an email to cookingchallenge@fake.com. As always, we appreciate each and every one of our contestants. We hope to ensure your safety and joy by providing the following rules to all contestants, taste testers, and observers:

  • Display any allergen information, GMO, vegan, vegetarian, or other dietary information that could be considered pertinent on your dishes presentation card. Taste testers will need this to ensure we have no allergen related accidents.
  • Respect all other contestants. Abusive language of any kind will not be tolerated. If you have an issue let the food do the talking.
  • Have fun! We hope this event will bring together a wide variety of people. If you're looking to learn how to cook, make friends, or just try some tasty dishes, make sure you're enjoying yourself!

The staff at Cohagan Cooking Challenge is always happy to answer any questions that you may have. If you need to report an incident, get help finding your booth, or check in on taste testing times please seek out any staff member. We'll get you situated.
